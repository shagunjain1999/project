Our Mission

Mission is to create, provide and expand positively life-changing experiences through music for all. We believe that music and arts education is a core to students' education and must be available to all students. Music for All is an advocate for music and arts education.

Vision

We will use our resources to provide national programs that recognize and support music students' performance and success, offer music educator training and professional development, and deliver tools and resources to participants and their communities that will assist them in supporting music education by promoting awareness of music’s impact on student growth and achievemen.
